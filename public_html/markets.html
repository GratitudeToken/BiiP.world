Default settings for Share2Earn algoritm:
50% of tokens earned through sharing goes into like2give balance and 50% you can take home.
50% of likes you get you can take home, 50% go straight to your Like2Give Balance.
50% goes to user that shares and brings exposure and the other 50% goes to his Like2Give balance.
The basic starting amount of tokens spent per user share action is decided freely by the market average.
However, authors may tilt the balance in either direction up to 100%.

If for example the author decides to give 80% of the tokens to the user that shared, this is seen as selfish, because that leaves only 20% for the Like2Give balance where he can distribute the tokens through liking other people's posts. In this case, the amount of tokens per share any user earns also decreases by the additional 30% (the multiplication factor here is per follower and other criteria).

If the balance is tilted the other way, 80% for the Like2Give balance, this is seen as altruistic and helping distribute wealth uniformly, so the basic amount per share also increases by 30% compared to average market price.
